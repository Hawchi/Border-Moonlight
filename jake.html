ABOUT

Jake is ENHYPEN’s golden retriever energy! Hailing from Brisbane, Australia, he acts as the group’s radiant light, bringing a warm and passionate spirit to every stage. Despite training for less than a year after joining via a global audition, his immense dedication led him to place #3 on I-LAND. He is celebrated for his rich, deep vocals and a magnetic stage charisma that balances his sweet, energetic off-stage personality. Bound by fate and a heart of gold, Jake is the loyal guardian of his members.
