ABOUT

Park Sunghoon is ENHYPEN’s "Ice Prince" with a surprisingly warm heart. Hailing from Namyangju, he brought years of professional figure skating discipline to the stage, transforming athletic precision into graceful idol charisma. After ranking #6 on I-LAND, he became known for his striking visuals and calm, observant personality. Though he may appear cool and collected, Sunghoon possesses an unexpectedly witty charm that shines once he warms up to his surroundings. From the frozen rink to the moonlit stage, he embodies focus, elegance, and undeniable main character energy.
