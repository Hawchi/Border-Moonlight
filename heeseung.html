ABOUT

Heeseung is the all-rounder ace of Enhypen! Born on October 15, 2001.
He’s known for his angelic vocals, balanced visuals and effortless stage charisma. Before debut, he trained for over 3 years under BigHit and was praised as a top trainee. He placed #5 on I-LAND and is loved for his calming presence, reliable energy and his brother vibe. Off stage, he’s chill, witty and a little shy – but always loveable!
