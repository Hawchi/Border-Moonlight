ABOUT

Ni-ki is ENHYPEN’s "Dance Machine" and the group's formidable Golden Maknae. Hailing from Okayama, Japan, he brought a legendary reputation as a dance prodigy to the moonlit stage, possessing precision and charisma far beyond his years. Ranking #4 on I-LAND, he consistently impressed fans and mentors alike with his relentless performance energy and technical mastery. Off stage, Ni-ki maintains a chill, witty, and somewhat mysterious aura; he is playful with his members yet remains deeply quiet and observant. ENGENEs are captivated by his sharp duality—cool and intense under the spotlight, yet soft and calm when the cameras fade.
