ABOUT

Kim Sunoo is ENHYPEN’s undeniable sunshine, radiating a sparkle energy that lights up the moonlit stage. Hailing from Suwon, he is beloved for his bright smile, expressive facial features, and a natural charm that draws everyone in. Despite training for less than a year, he ranked #8 on I-LAND, proving his immense stage charisma and vocal talent to the world. Off stage, Sunoo is cheerful, playful, and full of personality—the kind of member who uplifts everyone around him with laughter and winks. Adored by ENGENEs for his perfect balance of "soft and sassy," he remains a vital, glowing presence in the group.
