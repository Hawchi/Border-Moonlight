Storyworld

ENHYPEN Lore

Enhypen's lore is built around the idea of boys who transform into vampires
as part of a mysterious destiny. It's tied to the dark moon universe, where they exist as vampire characters with deeper backstories.

Phase 1

Awakening

The first shiver feels like light and shadow breathing together. No choice yet, only the sense that one must be made. A line forms between trainee and artist, human and something beyond, night and the first light of dawn.

“When the moon touches the glass, you don’t see yourself. You see who you can become.”

  • Theme Fate against choice, the moment one crosses into a new self.
  • Blood Moon

Intro: Walk the Line

  • Crossing the line begins a process of connection and rebirth

  • Transformation: Desire and fear merge to form a new identity
  • Key symbols: Blood, mirrors, red light, chains, reflection.
  • Theme: The tension between fate that is given and fate that is taken

Phase 2

The Craving

The boys awaken in a bright, distorted world. Desire takes hold, where youth and illusion fade into one. The light they followed blinds them now, and joy turns into obsession.

“Drunk on the light, lost in the dream. Where does desire end, and loss begin?”

  • Title track Drunk-Dazed
  • Concepts UP · HYPE · DOWN
  • Theme Youth intoxication, where celebration slips into chaos.
  • Lights & Illusions
  • Masked Identity
  • Craving & Confusion
  • Blood and Glamour
  • Duality of Celebration

BORDER: CARNIVAL

  • Release date: April 26, 2021
  • Title track: Drunk-Dazed
  • Concepts: Up · Hype · Down
  • Tracks: Intro – The Invitation · Drunk-Dazed · Fever · Not For Sale · Mixed Up · Outro – The Wormhole

Fever

  • Explores themes of helpless passion and temptation, showing how desire can consume and control
  • They try to break free from their powers, but the darkness keeps holding them back
  • Their bodies burn with desire, but the only warmth they feel is in each other’s arms
  • References their cold bodies, symbolizing a longing for recognition and warmth from humans
  • Symbol: Vampires unable to endure the warmth of humans

Drunk-Dazed

  • General concept: The members are vampires hosting a party to lure and obtain blood
  • Key symbols: Blood as alcohol, mirrors, fountains, eternal youth
  • Meaning: Addiction, something that brings pleasure yet leads to destruction
  • Visual motifs: Selen, the girl who transforms them into vampires
  • Lyric echo: “I’m addicted, I replay, play, play.”
  • Fountain scene: Blood poured into a fountain, symbolizing youth and immortality

Youth vs Instinct

The fever fades, but the struggle stays. Youth and instinct pull them apart. One leads toward control, the other toward desire. They begin to wonder what growing up means when every choice demands something in return.

Phase 5

Sacrifice and Fate

The bond between desire and destiny deepens. The boys find themselves tied to a fate they cannot escape — bound by sacrifice, blood, and devotion. To save what they love, they must give up a part of themselves.

“I offered my heart — not for power, but to keep you alive.”

  • Title track Bite Me
  • Concepts FULL · HALF · NEW
  • Theme Love and sacrifice — the eternal bond of fate and devotion
  • Blood Bond

Concept Trailer · Dark Blood

  • The trailer explores themes of immortality, devotion, and the cost of love.
  • The boys are bound by blood, symbolizing connection and eternal fate.
  • Light and darkness coexist — salvation and curse become one.
  • Represents the transition from innocence to acceptance of destiny.
  • “The deeper the bond, the heavier the sacrifice.”

DARK BLOOD

  • Release date: May 22, 2023
  • Title track: Bite Me
  • Concepts: Full · Half · New
  • Tracks: Fate · Bite Me · Sacrifice (Eat Me Up) · Chaconne · Bills · Karma

Fate

  • Concept: The intertwining of destiny and affection — eternal connection through blood.
  • Visuals: Dimly lit rooms, glowing red hues, and shattered reflections.
  • Meaning: The price of love is eternal bondage; salvation through surrender.
  • Key line: “If it’s for you, I’ll bleed forever.”

Sacrifice (Eat Me Up)

  • Concept: The act of giving up oneself — body and soul — to protect another.
  • Visuals: Flames, pale light, and a blood-red moon — the merging of pain and desire.
  • Meaning: Sacrifice as a twisted form of love and redemption.
  • Theme: The curse of devotion, where loss becomes proof of loyalty.

Bite Me

  • General Concept: Connection, fate, and sacrifice through the lens of a vampire narrative.
  • Meaning: A reunion with a destined love interest — acknowledging their bond through a bite.
  • Setting: Roman ruins and candlelit halls, creating a dark and mysterious atmosphere.
  • Choreography: Surrounded by female dancers representing temptation and ritual binding.
  • Symbolism: The bite marks the acceptance of destiny — no longer resisting fate.
  • Theme: Eternal bonds through blood — love becomes both salvation and curse.

Phase 6

Poison

“Love tastes like poison — sweet at first, but burning forever after.”

Phase 7

Dark Moon

The story stretches across centuries — a 200-year loop of loyalty, betrayal and rebirth. The boys become entangled with vampires, hunters and a girl who keeps appearing in every era. Time itself starts to blur: memories feel like déjà-vu, and fate feels like a script they’ve read before.

“If time keeps rewinding — is this devotion or a curse I can never wake up from?”

Quick Facts

  • Era MEMORABILIA (2024)
  • Title track “Fatal Trouble”
  • Concepts MOON · VAGR
  • Theme 200-year cycle of love, guilt and supernatural fate
  • 200-Year Time Loop
  • Train Journey Through Time
  • Moonlit Cityscapes
  • Secret Recordings & Cameras
  • Blurring Line Between Memory & Reality

MEMORABILIA

  • Release date: May 13, 2024
  • Title track: Fatal Trouble
  • Concepts: Moon & Vagr
  • Tracks: One In A Billion · Criminal Love · Fatal Trouble · Teeth · Lucifer · Scream · Dark Moon Special Album

Fatal Trouble

  • General concept: A mysterious train journey through time and trauma.
  • Setting: Burning nightlife, cold alleys and flickering neon over the city.
  • Key symbols: Tickets, cameras, fireworks, crowded trains, escape scenes.
  • Meaning: Being trapped between guilt and devotion — choosing whether to break the cycle or repeat it again.

Dark Moon Storyline

  • The boys are tied to vampires, hunters and a girl who keeps reappearing.
  • Each “life” feels like a fragment of a bigger, looping narrative.
  • The moon becomes a witness — watching every choice across centuries.
  • Fate asks: are they protecting her, or repeating the same mistake over and over?
