The Origin

CONNECT: THE HYPHEN

"Like the meaning of the hyphen (-), our name signifies seven members intertwined as they search for themselves and grow together, connecting people and worlds."

Formed through the intense survival journey of I-LAND, ENHYPEN has emerged as a global powerhouse. Their identity is defined by a continuous narrative of awakening from the royal mysteries of the BORDER era to the complex ambitions of DIMENSION and the primal instincts found in DARK BLOOD.

  • ✦ Identity: Seven souls bound by fate to redefine cinematic storytelling in music.
  • ✦ The Storyworld: A seamless journey through BORDER, DIMENSION, MANIFESTO, and the BLOOD chronicles.
  • ✦ Members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Ni-ki.
  • ✦ I-LAND Legacy: From applicants to global icons, connecting ENGENE across every border.
