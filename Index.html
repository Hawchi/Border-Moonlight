Lore

ENHYPEN Storyworld

Dark Moon

A supernatural world that expands ENHYPEN’s narrative. It introduces characters, symbols, and events that return throughout music videos, concept films, and the Dark Moon webtoon. The story explores themes of fate, temptation, inner conflict, and identity.

Dimension Era

A chapter focused on identity, growth, and ambition. As the storyline progresses from BORDER to DIMENSION, the members enter a world shaped by desire, expectation, and personal freedom. This era represents their journey from youth into a more complex stage of self-awareness.

Symboles & Codes

ENHYPEN’s universe uses recurring visual motifs such as the moon, mirrors, blood, light, and shadow. These symbols form a consistent visual language that appears in styling, set design, album concepts, and choreography. They connect the eras and strengthen the group’s overarching narrative world.

About ENHYPEN

Group Profile

ENHYPEN is a seven-member group formed through the survival show I-LAND. Their identity centers around cinematic storytelling, symbolic worldbuilding, and concepts that evolve across albums. From fate and awakening to desire, youth, instinct, sacrifice, and rebellion, their work unfolds as a continuous narrative that connects music, visuals, and lore.

  • Members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Ni-ki
  • Focus: Narrative-driven concepts, performance, and symbolic visual themes
  • Storyworld: BORDER → DIMENSION → DARK BLOOD → ORANGE BLOOD → MEMORABILIA
  • Highlights: Global tours, award-winning performances, expanding interconnected lore
